About

Built for café lovers, by a café lover

SipSpot started as a personal need. I kept discovering great cafés but had no reliable way to remember which ones had fast Wi-Fi, plenty of outlets, or the right energy for a focused work session. Google reviews are helpful, but they don't answer the questions that matter most to people who actually work in cafés.


So I built SipSpot: a community-driven app where every rating is specific, every review is from someone who cares about the craft, and every saved spot is a little piece of someone's daily routine.
