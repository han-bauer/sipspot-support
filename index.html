Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a cafe to my favorites or wishlist?

Open any cafe's detail page and tap the heart icon to favorite it, or the bookmark icon to add it to your wishlist.

How do I find cafes near me?

Open the Map or Explore tab. SipSpot uses your location to show cafes nearby. Make sure location access is enabled in your iPhone's Settings → SipSpot.

How do I add friends?

Go to your Profile tab and tap the friends icon. Search for friends by username and send them a friend request.

How do I edit or delete a review?

Navigate to your Profile → Reviews tab, find the review you'd like to change, and tap the edit or delete option.

My location isn't working. What should I do?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → SipSpot and make sure it's set to "While Using the App." Then reopen SipSpot.
